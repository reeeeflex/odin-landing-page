This is a Tanjiro Kamado Fanpage

Tanjiro was born on a mountain as the eldest son of Kie and Tanjuro Kamado, a family of charcoal sellers. After his five younger siblings were born, he adopted the idea that as the oldest child, he should be strong and endure any hardships for them. Although the family did not enjoy a lavish lifestyle, they lived happily and showed a great deal of love and care for one another. When Takeo accidentally knocked over a hot kettle, Tanjiro jumped in to protect him, resulting in the burn scar on his forehead.
